About Us

IEEE-Computer Society is a student chapter in VIT University, Vellore.
Founded in 2012 by a group of tech enthusiasts, the the chapter has been on an amazing journey ever since! Our chapter's mission is to foster innovation, learning and networking in the most happening areas of computing technologies and we have been doing so by conducting various workshops and events throughout the semester over the years.
